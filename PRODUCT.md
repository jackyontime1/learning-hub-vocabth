# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Thai learners who read English on phones and desktop browsers. They need a calm place to read at the right CEFR level, check Thai meaning without losing context, and save useful vocabulary for later review.

## Product Purpose

Daily English Reader turns free, quality-checked news and curated daily-life practice stories into dependable bilingual reading practice. Success means learners can understand a complete story, inspect unfamiliar words, notice useful phrases, and return each day without the interface competing for attention.

## Brand Personality

Calm, trustworthy, practical, and learner-friendly. The voice is clear and editorial, with quiet confidence rather than marketing polish.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS landing pages and startup dashboards.
- Purple or blue gradients, glassmorphism, glow effects, and decorative icon tiles.
- Excessive cards, nested cards, and full-page highlighting that makes reading visually noisy.
- Tiny gray text, weak contrast, ornamental motion, and over-polished promotional copy.

## Design Principles

- Put the reading task first; controls should recede until needed.
- Keep English and Thai comfortable to scan with generous line height and restrained line length.
- Reveal learning help in context without interrupting reading flow.
- Use familiar, accessible controls and clear interaction states.
- Preserve the distinction between planned practice stories and real news.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for WCAG AA contrast and interaction decisions. Maintain visible keyboard focus, semantic headings, 44px touch targets where practical, reduced-motion support, straightforward navigation, and mobile layouts that do not require horizontal scrolling. English and Thai body text must remain comfortably readable without relying on color alone.
